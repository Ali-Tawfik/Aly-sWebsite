Machine Learning and Data analysis (Software)
During my work term at Neuruno I’ve worked with Python to store and analyse spectral data. By analysing the stored data using various types of SVMs and Neural networks, I was able to create a model to predict the amount of content inside of different products. I’ve also worked on assessing the accuracy of the model using R2 testing and displaying results using different types of graphs.This was all done in Python using Pandas Dataframe with matplotlib,TensorFlow and sklearn.
Auxillius (Software/Hardware)
This project was designed by a group of 2 people during a compitition at The Forge in Hamilton. Our solution was designed to tackle multiple problems regarding early release of patiances from St Josph hospital. I was responsible for using an Iot device and working with the patient side. The iot device was hooked up to a raspberry pie mini to read the heartbeat of the patient. The information was then uploaded to a Node.js local server, where the information could be extracted and displayed in a live graph using the Auxilius app. This app was designed as a proof of concept regarding our solution.
Myo Band Rc-Car (Software/Hardware)
During my first highschool hackathon, Massey Hacks my team and I decided to create a hardware hack. Our hack involved the use of a myo band to control an RC car with servos and motors. The data was sent through bluetooth to the laptop, with an arduino uno and some programming in C we were able to control the movement of the RC car with regards to the myo band. Creating a fist would cause the car to move backwards, an open palm makes it move forward and open palm pointed to the left or right would make the car move left or right respectively.
